About Us


Aihika is a farm product,tools buying and selling system.Aihika is established with an intention to encourage growth and intake of organic products. As many farmers who are doing contemporary farming are in fact getting aware of the importance of Organic cultivation and Natural farming, but are hesitant to adapt those methods, we wanted to go in aid of them.

Sellers or farmers can create their own account after uploading their details and rtc documentand it gets validated. Sellers can use this account to sell their farm products ,tools ,fertilizers, pesticides etc.

Buyer can create their account and login to search for and buy organic products and tools in and around them. Information related to each crop like the day of harvest,fertilizers used,weather of cultivation will be provided. Buyers can then place an order for their produce or products and pick it up from the destined place.

Aihika provides a platform for farmers to sell their produce directly to customers and buyers can buy organic products wthout any middlemen involved.
